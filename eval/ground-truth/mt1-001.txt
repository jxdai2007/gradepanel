Problem 1 (10 pts)

Find d/dx [sin(2x²)] using the chain rule.

Student answer:
Let u = 2x², so f(u) = sin(u).
By the chain rule: d/dx [sin(2x²)] = cos(2x²) · d/dx [2x²]
d/dx [2x²] = 4x
Therefore d/dx [sin(2x²)] = 4x cos(2x²).
